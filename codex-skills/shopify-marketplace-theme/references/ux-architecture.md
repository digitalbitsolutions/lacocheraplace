# Marketplace UX Architecture

Move the store from a product-catalog mentality to a service-marketplace mentality.

## Desired signals

- Services first
- Providers or workshops visible
- Clear CTAs such as reserve, request, compare, or contact
- Trust and guidance before transaction complexity

## Typical homepage order

1. Hero with clear service promise
2. Service categories
3. How it works
4. Providers or workshops
5. Social proof
6. Final CTA

## Elements to reduce or replace

- Generic featured products
- Cart-first messaging
- Inventory-first language for service flows
